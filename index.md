# Privacy Policy for iPhone Trackpad

Effective date: June 7, 2026

iPhone Trackpad is an app that lets an iPhone control a Mac over your local Wi-Fi network using the companion Mac receiver app, iPhone Trackpad Receiver.

## Overview

iPhone Trackpad is designed to work locally between your iPhone and your Mac.

The app does not create user accounts, does not sell data, does not use advertising tracking, and does not use third-party analytics.

## Local Network Communication

iPhone Trackpad communicates with iPhone Trackpad Receiver over your local Wi-Fi network.

This local connection is used to send trackpad actions to your Mac, including pointer movement, clicks, scrolling, keyboard commands, and voice text.

This information is sent to the Mac receiver app on your own local network. It is not sent to our servers.

## Microphone and Speech Recognition

iPhone Trackpad includes an optional voice input feature. The microphone is used only when you tap the microphone button.

When you use voice input, the app uses Apple’s speech recognition features to convert your speech into text. The recognized text is then sent over your local network to iPhone Trackpad Receiver so it can be typed into the active text field on your Mac.

iPhone Trackpad does not record, store, sell, or share your voice audio.

## Mac Accessibility Permission

iPhone Trackpad Receiver needs macOS Accessibility permission so it can move the pointer, click, scroll, press keys, and type text on your Mac.

This permission is used only to provide the trackpad and input features of the app.

## App Settings

iPhone Trackpad stores app settings on your device, such as pointer sensitivity, scroll speed, haptic settings, brightness preference, and which optional controls are enabled.

These settings stay on your device.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your data.

We do not share your personal information with advertisers or data brokers.

The app does not use advertising tracking or third-party analytics.

## Children’s Privacy

iPhone Trackpad is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this page.
